# Forbidden Words & Secular-Sublime Voice Guide

**Owner:** Brand & Marketing
**Status:** Active — enforced across all user-facing copy

---

## Theophany Mode — Forbidden

Avoid these words entirely:

- haunted
- sacred
- proven
- confirmed
- verified *(in the supernatural sense)*
- "it is known"
- blessed
- holy *(as a descriptor for a place)*
- cursed

**Preferred alternatives:** encounter, threshold, reported, felt by others, visitors describe, some have noted, accounts suggest, people have said

---

## Sanctuary Mode — Forbidden

Avoid these words entirely:

- woo
- supernatural
- spooky
- ghostly
- "energy" *(new age sense)*
- haunted
- vibe *(as a spiritual claim)*
- vortex
- portal

**Preferred alternatives:** stillness, refuge, quiet, rest, contemplation, peace, gathered silence, atmosphere, weight of the place

---

## For the "Nones" (30% of Americans)

Secular-sublime vocabulary for non-religious users describing profound experiences. These words are always acceptable:

- awe
- dread
- uncanniness
- stillness
- atmosphere
- weight of the place
- time moves differently here
- I couldn't explain it
- something shifted
- the silence felt full

---

## Enforcement

All place descriptions, onboarding copy, marketing materials, and in-app text must pass this list before publication. When in doubt, ask: *does this make a metaphysical claim or merely report a human experience?*
