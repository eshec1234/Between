# Brand Voice Guide

**Owner:** Brand & Marketing

---

## Voice in One Sentence

Between speaks with the quiet authority of a witness, not the enthusiasm of a salesperson.

---

## Tone Dimensions

| Dimension | Yes | No |
|---|---|---|
| Register | Measured, contemplative | Excited, promotional |
| Certainty | Testimony, report | Claim, proof |
| Warmth | Understated, present | Effusive, performative |
| Complexity | Comfortable with ambiguity | Reductive, explanatory |
| Humor | Dry, rare | Irreverent about the sacred |

---

## The Secular-Sublime Voice

Between must be legible to:
- The devout Catholic
- The secular Jew
- The agnostic who cries at sunsets
- The skeptic who still won't go to a certain building alone

This requires language that reports without interpreting. "People have felt something here" is always safer than "this place is holy."

---

## Mode-Specific Voice

### Sanctuary
- Warm but not soft
- Quiet without being hushed
- Words: refuge, gathered, still, held, slow

### Theophany
- Alert, not alarmed
- Strange without sensationalizing
- Words: threshold, encounter, weight, uncanny, unresolved
