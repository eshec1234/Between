# Partner Custodians — Alternative Ad Model

**Owner:** Business Operations
**Status:** Framework established; partners TBD

---

## Philosophy

Between does not run ads. It partners with **custodians** — local businesses whose existence is itself a form of what Between maps.

A monastic bakery, a silent bookshop, a contemplative tea house — these are not advertisers. They are adjacent expressions of the same human need Between serves.

---

## What a Partner Custodian Is

A local business that:

1. Embodies quiet or contemplative practice as part of its identity
2. Does not require spiritual affiliation to enter
3. Would be discovered naturally by a Between user
4. Is not optimizing for scale or national reach

---

## What a Partner Custodian Is NOT

- A wellness brand
- A meditation app or tech product
- A national chain with a "mindfulness" marketing angle
- A business that sells access to spiritual experience

---

## Listing Format

Partner custodians appear as a separate section in the app — clearly labeled "Places to Rest Nearby" or similar — distinct from the place database. They are static listings, not algorithmically promoted.

**No click tracking. No impression metrics. No conversion data.**

Revenue model: flat monthly listing fee, to be determined.

---

## Target Partner Categories

| Category | Example |
|---|---|
| Monastic bakeries | Monastery bread sales open to public |
| Independent bookshops | Shops with contemplative or religious sections |
| Tea houses / coffee shops | Those known for quiet atmosphere |
| Used bookshops | Particularly those with religion/philosophy sections |
| Small gardens / nurseries | Adjacent to listed places |
| Independent chapels / meditation centers | Non-commercial, drop-in welcome |
