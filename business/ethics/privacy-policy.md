# Privacy Policy

**Effective:** Week 5
**Owner:** Ethics Division

---

## What We Collect

### What we DO collect
- A randomly generated anonymous session ID stored in your browser's localStorage
- Place submissions you choose to submit (name, address, description)
- Experience reports you choose to submit (text content, optional reflection tag)

### What we DO NOT collect
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number
- Your location (we ask for GPS access to center the map — this is never stored or transmitted)
- Your browsing history
- Any persistent identifier linked to you personally

---

## Anonymous by Design

Between's anonymity is architectural, not a setting. There is no account to create, no profile to manage, and no way to connect your session ID to your identity. Your session ID resets if you clear browser storage.

---

## Third-Party Services

Between uses:
- **Supabase** — database hosting (submissions are stored here)
- **Mapbox** — map rendering (Mapbox receives your approximate location when you use the map)
- **Vercel** — hosting (standard server logs apply)

We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or social media pixels.

---

## Data Retention

- Session IDs: retained until you clear localStorage
- Submissions: retained indefinitely unless removed per content policy
- Server logs: per Vercel's standard retention policy

---

## Your Rights

You have no account to delete. If you submitted content you wish removed, contact us via [contact method TBD]. We will remove submissions on request where technically feasible.
