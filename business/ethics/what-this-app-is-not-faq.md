# What This App Is Not — FAQ

**Owner:** Ethics Division
**Status:** Active — may be surfaced in onboarding or About page

---

## What is Between?

Between is a map of places where humans have reported feeling something — awe, dread, stillness, uncanniness, or peace — without telling you what to believe about those experiences.

---

## What Between is NOT

### Not a spiritual authority

Between does not adjudicate spiritual experience. A place appearing in Between does not mean it is sacred, blessed, or spiritually significant in any objective sense. It means people have reported feeling something there.

### Not a paranormal validator

Between does not confirm, verify, or endorse supernatural claims. Experience reports in Theophany mode reflect what users have reported. Between makes no determination about the nature of those experiences.

### Not a replacement for religious community

Between is not a church, sangha, mosque, synagogue, or any other form of gathered religious community. It does not provide pastoral care, spiritual direction, or communal practice.

### Not a digital shrine

Places in Between are not memorialized, consecrated, or archived as sacred sites. Listings can be removed. The database is not a permanent record of spiritual geography.

### Not a therapy tool

Between is not a mental health resource. If you are in crisis, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988) or go to your nearest emergency room.

### Not an expert system

Between does not employ religious scholars, spiritual directors, or paranormal investigators to verify content. All place descriptions are human-reported and should be understood as personal testimony.

---

## Who should use Between?

Anyone who has ever felt something in a place they couldn't explain — and wanted to know if others had felt it too.

No belief required.
