# Metaphysical Drift Check

**Owner:** Ethics Division
**Cadence:** Weekly — every sprint review
**Format:** Standing agenda item, 5 minutes

---

## The Weekly Question

> "Does any decision made this week unintentionally create sacred authority, a metaphysical claim, or a ritual structure?"

---

## What to Look For

### Sacred Authority Drift
- Does any new feature imply that Between validates or ranks spiritual experiences?
- Does any copy suggest that Between's curation makes a place more significant?
- Are we using words like "curated", "selected", or "recommended" in ways that imply spiritual judgment?

### Metaphysical Claim Drift
- Has any place description slipped into asserting rather than reporting?
- Has AI-generated content appeared in user-facing views? (See ai-behind-curtain-policy.md)
- Are forbidden words appearing in the UI? (See forbidden-words.md)

### Ritual Structure Drift
- Has any feature introduced a repeating behavior pattern (streaks, check-ins, return prompts)?
- Are we accidentally creating a liturgical calendar or practice rhythm?
- Has gamification appeared in any form?

## Escalation

If drift is confirmed:

1. Pause the feature or copy in question
2. Document in this log
3. Bring to Ethics division within 48 hours
4. Document resolution in risk-register.md
