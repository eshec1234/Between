# Content Policy

**Owner:** Ethics Division
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Place Submissions

### Accepted
- Any location where a human has genuinely felt awe, dread, peace, stillness, or uncanniness
- Active places of worship (with sensitivity fields completed)
- Historical sites, cemeteries, natural landscapes, built environments
- Privately owned spaces where public access exists

### Rejected
- Fictional locations
- Sites submitted with intent to harass or defame
- Indigenous or tribal land sites without explicit consent (see indigenous-land-policy.md)
- Sites that exist solely to promote a commercial business
- Locations with AI-generated descriptions (see ai-behind-curtain-policy.md)

---

## Experience Reports

### Accepted
- Personal testimony describing felt experience
- Descriptions of atmosphere, sensation, or perception
- Honest negative reports ("I felt nothing", "It was just a building")

### Rejected
- AI-generated content
- Hate speech or targeting of religious/ethnic groups
- Harassment of specific individuals associated with a place
- Spam or promotional content
- Claims presented as objective fact ("This place is definitely haunted")

---

## Moderation Approach

Between uses a combination of:
1. Community flagging
2. AI-assisted triage (internal only — see ai-behind-curtain-policy.md)
3. Human review for flagged content

Moderation decisions are final. Appeal process TBD.
